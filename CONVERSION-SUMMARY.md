## 🚀 Quick Start

### Minimum Steps to Get Running

1. **Upload the theme folder** to `wp-content/themes/`
2. **Activate** the theme in WordPress admin
3. **Create a page** called "Home"
4. **Set it as homepage** in Settings → Reading
5. **Configure contact info** in Appearance → Customize

That's it! Your site is ready.
